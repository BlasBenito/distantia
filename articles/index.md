### Original Paper

- [Benito & Birks 2020
  (Ecography)](https://nsojournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/ecog.04895):

  This paper introduces ‘distantia’, an R package to assess
  dissimilarity between regular or irregular, univariate or multivariate
  time-series
